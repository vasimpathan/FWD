FlightCare Flight

Full coverage details

Key features

  1. Provide HK$500,000 coverage on accidental death while insured is on flight. Note that the accident needs to be visible and causing bodily injuries

  2. Coverage will be added on top of any existing cover the insured has

  3. Insured can be protected by 1 flight insurance policy per trip only. If Insured are covered by multiple flight insurance policies by FWD, only HK$500,000 coverage

  4. No coverage while the insured is not on flight

  5. Cover Journey to and from Hong Kong that is within 30 days

  6. Cover all flights during the Journey (including connecting flights) as a fare paying passenger, but excludes a contractor, chartered or private carriers, and any other carriers which are operated primarily for sight-seeing service and amusement of the passenger

Eligibility

  • All Hong Kong ID card holder
  • Age of 6 weeks up to a maximum age of 85 years
  • Exclude crew member or an operator of any air carrier, tour guide or tour escort, and armed force services.

Major Terms & Conditions:

  1. Applicable to HKID cardholders only.
  2. Maximum 30 days' roundtrip coverage.
  3. Eligible for trip that departs within 30 days from the application date.
  4. Applicable to trips originating from and returning to Hong Kong only.
  5. Maximum coverage per insured life for FlightCare is HK$500,000 regardless of the number of FlightCare policies applied.
  6. Not Applicable to air crews and tour guides.
